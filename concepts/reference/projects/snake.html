Snake Gambling

Classic Snake with a Gambling Twist

Open Source

Overview

A modern twist on the classic Snake game that combines traditional gameplay with gambling and shop features. Collect eggs, upgrade your snake, and try your luck in the gambling section! Built with Python and Pygame.

Key Features

  • Classic Gameplay: Traditional snake mechanics with modern graphics.
  • Gambling System: Wheel of Fortune and Slots mini-games.
  • Shop & Upgrades: Spend earned points on snake upgrades.
